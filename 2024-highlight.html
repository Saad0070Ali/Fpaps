FPAPS 2024: Event Highlights & Impact

The 2024 Fiji Physical Activity Promotion Symposium (FPAPS), held at the University of Fiji, brought together 170 delegates for an inspiring day of knowledge sharing and collaboration. The event featured 2 keynote addresses, 5 student presentations, 2 invited talks, and an engaging panel discussion. With the support of sponsors, the symposium spotlighted innovative perspectives on physical activity, including powerful student-led insights and dynamic Taekwondo demonstrations.

A remarkable 94% of attendees reported being extremely satisfied or satisfied, with 100% stating the event was well-organized and worth attending again. Attendees especially appreciated the demonstrations, audience interaction, and quality of student contributions. Based on this overwhelming success, the organizing committee looks forward to an even more inclusive and engaging FPAPS 2025!
